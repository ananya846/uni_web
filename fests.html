Fests & Activities at BMSCE
BMSCE runs two major annual fests that bring the entire campus alive. Switch to fest mode using the admin bar above during the season.
⚡
PhaseShift
Annual Technical & Cultural Fest · April
BMSCE's flagship technical fest drawing 10,000+ participants from across Karnataka. Includes hackathons, robotics competitions, paper presentations, DJ night, and celebrity performances.
🎪
Utsav
Annual Cultural Fest · February
BMSCE's cultural celebration with dance competitions, dramatics, art exhibitions, fashion shows, and music. A 3-day event that showcases the non-technical talents of BMSCE students.
